Mini Music Player:



While it is an Utility application, the entertainment features such as Music and games should be there. Here, this is our Mini-Music player in the application.

Build : Nothing more like the previous one, it is built to play music in the local storage and will be your first course of action when you are free or in works.

Advantages: A Light weight music player which has its own UI Design for Windows and Mobile views may make the moment your's.

Disadvantages: As it has only the local storage access, it can't play music from online. But, it will be enabled in future.
